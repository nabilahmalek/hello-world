ABOUT US

Uncle Best Fried Chicken is a famous fried chicken franchise among UiTM Kedah students. This franchise is located in UiTM Kedah and is conducted by Tokey Kek Confections. This is a sole-proprietorship, run by Puan Anis as the owner-manager. With effect from January 2018, the company secured the right to be a stockist for Uncle Best Mart franchise in Kedah and Perlis. Since then, the company's main focus has shifted from the bakery business to the fried chicken franchise. For now, they operate daily at a kiosk within UiTM Kedah and weekly at a FAMA farmer's market at Bandar Puteri Jaya, Sungai Petani.
